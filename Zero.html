Zero, his backstory, and why he is Top 3 for favorite siege operators

Zero and what he offers as an operator

Zero (real name Sam Fisher) is an Attacker operator introduced in Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege during Operation Shadow Legacy (Year 5, Season 3). He is a seasoned veteran and a former member of Third Echelon, a covert organization from the Splinter Cell series, which makes him one of the most tactical and stealth-oriented characters in the game. Zero has access to the SC3000K assault rifle or the MP7 submachine gun. The SC3000K is a solid mid-range weapon, offering a good balance of damage and accuracy, while the MP7 provides high rate of fire, making it excellent for close-quarters combat. Zero has the 5.7 USG or the Gonne-6 for his secondaries, and Hard Breachers or Claymores.

Zero's description and accomplishments

Zero (real name Sam Fisher) is a seasoned veteran and a key Attacker operator in Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege, introduced in Operation Shadow Legacy (Year 5, Season 3). He is a former member of Third Echelon, a covert division of the NSA, and a well-known character from the Splinter Cell series. His expertise in stealth operations, intelligence gathering, and tactical combat makes him a valuable addition to Rainbow Six Siege's roster. Sam Fisher's career spans decades of service in various high-stakes operations. He was a prominent figure in Splinter Cell, where he was an elite operative working for Third Echelon. Fisher is known for his stealth, precision, and ability to gather vital intelligence from hostile environments. Over his career, he became a legend in the intelligence community, often operating behind enemy lines without detection.

Why Zero is part of our Top 3 siege operators
